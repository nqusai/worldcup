📖 Game Rules (Click to expand)

Guess the final score of every match. The closer you are, the more points you get.

🔢 Scoring (3-2-1)

  • 🎯 3 Points: Exact final score (including EH and Penalties) (e.g. guessed 2-1, finished 2-1)
  • ⚖️ 2 Points: Correct winner + correct goal difference (e.g. guessed 3-1, finished 2-0)
  • ✅ 1 Point: Correct winner, wrong score (e.g. guessed 1-0, finished 4-0)
  • ❌ 0 Points: Wrong outcome

⏰ How to Play

  • 🔒 Predictions lock 1 hour before kickoff.
  • 👀 Open cards! You can see everyone else's guesses under each match.
  • ⏳ The app only shows matches happening in the next 72 hours.
  • ⏳ Final score refers to score after extra time or penalties if any.
